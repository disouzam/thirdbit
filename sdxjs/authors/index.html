Appendix G: Authors

Greg Wilson has worked in industry and academia for 35 years, and is the author, co-author, or editor of several books, including Beautiful Code, The Architecture of Open Source Applications, JavaScript for Data Science, Teaching Tech Together, and [Research Software Engineering with Python][py-rse]. He was the co-founder and first Executive Director of Software Carpentry and received ACM SIGSOFT's Influential Educator Award in 2020.
